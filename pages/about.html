About My MBTI

My MBTI is a premier destination for individuals seeking to understand themselves and their relationships better. Our mission is to provide high-quality, research-driven personality assessments that are accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or language.

Our Methodology

Our assessment is based on the four-letter personality typing system, exploring dimensions such as Extraversion vs. Introversion, Sensing vs. Intuition, Thinking vs. Feeling, and Judging vs. Perceiving. We combine traditional psychological frameworks with modern data analysis to ensure our results are both accurate and meaningful.

Why Choose Us?

  • Scientific Accuracy: We continuously refine our questions based on the latest psychological research.
  • Multilingual Support: We believe self-discovery should be accessible to all, offering our test in 5 major languages.
  • Privacy First: Your data stays with you. We prioritize user privacy and transparency in everything we do.

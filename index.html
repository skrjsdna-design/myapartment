Understanding the 4 Dimensions

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is based on Carl Jungâ€™s theory of psychological types. It identifies four pairs of opposite preferences, or "dimensions," which together form 16 unique personality types. Understanding these dimensions can help you navigate your career, relationships, and personal growth.

  • Extraversion (E) vs. Introversion (I): Where do you focus your attention and get your energy?
  • Sensing (S) vs. Intuition (N): How do you prefer to take in information?
  • Thinking (T) vs. Feeling (F): How do you make decisions?
  • Judging (J) vs. Perceiving (P): How do you deal with the outer world?
